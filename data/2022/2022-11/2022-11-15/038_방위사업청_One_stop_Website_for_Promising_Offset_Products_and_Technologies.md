## 본문

□ The Defense Aquisition Program Administration(DAPA) announced that to help promising domestic companies in various defense areas participate in the offset trade*, it expands the Offset Promising List and provides relevant information through the online exhibition hall(defense-korea.com) from Nov. 15.

 ㅇ The Offset Promising List is an easy-to-browse list of company information, products, and technical fields which is to support domestic defense companies’ participation in the offset trade.

 ㅇ which is to provide a list of preferred offset partners and items for foreign contractors to consider during their offset negotiations.
