## 본문

□ The 147th Defense Acquisition Program Executive Committee (DAPEC) was held virtually at 0930 in November 28, 2022.

□ Four topics were brought to the committee on this day.

 ① Basic Plan for Systems Development (draft) of 130mm Guided Rocket-II

 ② Basic Program Implementation Strategy (draft) for 155mm Precision Guided Ammunitions

 ③ Revised Basic Program Implementation Strategy (draft) and Purchase Plan (draft) for Heavy Attack Helicopter-II

 ④ Initial Production Plan for Light Armed Helicopter Program (draft)
